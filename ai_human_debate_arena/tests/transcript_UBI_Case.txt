I stand before you today to champion Universal Basic Income. We are facing a future where economic security is no longer guaranteed, and UBI is the lifeline we need. First, UBI eradicates poverty. The Roosevelt Institute found that a $1,000/month UBI would expand the economy by 12.56% and generate 4.6 million jobs. The World Bank reports 44% of Americans can't afford a $400 emergency. UBI changes that. Second, automation is displacing millions. McKinsey estimates 73 million U.S. jobs automated by 2030. UBI is a bridge for those cast aside. Third, UBI is freedom. It empowers workers to reject exploitation. A Harvard study shows workers with savings will accept 15% lower wages to escape toxic workplaces. This is about dignity. Don't let fearmongering about cost or work ethic distract you. We spend $1.1 trillion on welfare already. UBI is not a handout, it's an investment in human potential.
